Hello! I am Dr Felix M. Simon, a (political) communication researcher and the Research Fellow in AI, Information, and News at the Reuters Institute for the Study of Journalism within the Department of Politics at Oxford University. I am also a Research Associate at the Oxford Internet Institute and a Junior Research Fellow in Politics at Corpus Christi College, both at Oxford University. I research the implications of AI for information, news, and democracy. In a past life, I used to be a journalist.

My research broadly seeks to understand the structural implications of AI, including forms of generative AI, for the production, distribution, and consumption of information and news and the effects of AI on our information ecosystem (and by extension our societies).
