Consulting

I provide consulting services around the following topics: 1) Strategic overview of the AI landscape as it relates to news and journalism, based on the latest academic and industry evidence, 2) Help in dissecting industry trends and their ramifications around AI, 3) Workshops on questions of AI risks and benefits and adoption, especially with a view towards broader AI strategy, AI ethics, and relationships with technology companies, and 4) Bespoke consultations as required.

I can also be booked as a speaker for keynotes & panel events on topics such as AI, news and the information landscape.
